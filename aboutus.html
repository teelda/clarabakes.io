About Us

About Us

Clara's Bakes is a unique bakery known for our signature cupcakes and cakes. We take pride in using natural ingredients in our cupcakes, cakes, cookies, pies and desserts.
Our treats are as deliciously wholesome as they are beautifully decorated. Choose from our signature cupcakes, weekly and holiday specials or custom created desserts.

Our vision

Clara's Bakes offers a homestyle bakery experience in Calabar and nationwide. Our vision is to create upscale, quick-serve bakery with a focus on simple and satisfying the southern style desserts and baked goods.

Our mission

Clara's Bakes philosophy is to offer simple and delicious desserts that are easily accessible to clients via location or delivery options. Our ingredients are high quality; each dessert is carefully made with only the finest, all-natural ingredients.
